Our Story

About SwiftCargo

Built on decades of freight experience, we are the logistics partner importers trust to move their goods safely and on time.

Who We Are

A Logistics Company Built for Importers

SwiftCargo was founded to solve a specific problem: once an importer's container arrives at the port, the real logistical challenge begins. Coordinating unloading, sorting, storage, and onward transportation — across multiple carriers and modes — is complex and time-consuming.

We built our business to handle exactly that. Our facility (adda) serves as the central hub where containers are received, goods are carefully unloaded, and cargo is prepared for its next journey — whether by truck, train, or air.

With over 15 years of operational experience and a growing network of trusted partners, we deliver simplicity and reliability at every step. One point of contact. Many solutions.

"We don't just move cargo — we take on the responsibility of the entire journey, so our clients can focus on their business."

— The SwiftCargo Team
