Who We Are

A Logistics Company Built for Importers

Burraq Logistics was founded to solve a specific problem: once an importer's container arrives at port, the real logistical challenge begins. Coordinating unloading, sorting, storage, and onward transport across multiple carriers is complex, time-consuming, and expensive.

We built our business to handle exactly that. Our facility — the adda — serves as the central hub where containers are received, goods are carefully unloaded, and cargo is prepared for its next journey, whether by truck, train, or air.

With over 15 years of operational experience and a growing network of trusted partners, we deliver simplicity and reliability at every step. One point of contact. Many solutions.
