How Your Cargo Moves

A transparent, step-by-step look at exactly what happens from container arrival to final delivery.

Overview

One Process, Multiple Paths

Every freight journey with Burraq Logistics begins the same way: your container arrives at our central facility. From there, our team takes over — unloading, sorting, and preparing cargo based on destination and your preferred transport mode.

Whether road, railway, or air, the process is seamless. For door-to-door clients, we handle the entire chain from our adda to your doorstep.

Step by Step

The Full Journey

Every stage your cargo goes through — from container receipt to final signature at your destination.

Container Received

The importer's container arrives at our adda. Our team documents the arrival, inspects the seal, and logs the shipment before any unloading begins.

1
2

Cargo Unloaded at Adda

Our trained handlers carefully unload all goods. Items are sorted by destination, product type, and transport mode to prepare for dispatch.

Transport Mode Assigned

Based on the client's instructions and cargo type, the appropriate transport mode is confirmed and dispatch is scheduled.

3
4

Goods Loaded & Dispatched

Cargo is loaded onto the assigned vehicle or bogie and dispatched. For rail, this marks the official start of the freight service.

In Transit

Cargo is on the move. Our team monitors the shipment and coordinates with drivers, rail operators, or air handlers to keep everything on schedule.

5
6

Delivered & Confirmed

Cargo arrives at the client's factory, warehouse, or address. Delivery is confirmed, documentation completed, and the shipment is closed.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions importers ask us most often.

When does the freight service officially start?
+

For railway shipments, the freight service officially begins once goods are loaded into the bogies and the train departs. For road, it begins when the truck leaves our adda.

Do you handle the container unloading yourselves?
+

Yes. Once your container arrives at our adda, our team manages the entire unloading process. You do not need to be present — we document everything and keep you informed.

What types of goods do you transport?
+

We serve importers dealing in a wide range of products — raw materials, machinery, consumer goods, textiles, and general merchandise. Contact us for specific cargo requirements.

What is the container freight service exactly?
+

We receive your import container, unload the goods, then reload them into our own managed containers — giving us better control over handling, allowing consolidation or splitting as needed.

Can you deliver to any location in the country?
+

Yes. Our door-to-door service covers deliveries to factories, warehouses, or any address across Pakistan. Our road and rail networks give us wide geographic reach.

How do I get started or request a shipment?
+

Fill out the inquiry form on our Contact page or call us directly. Share your cargo details, preferred transport mode, and destination — we'll handle the rest.
