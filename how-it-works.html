How Your Cargo Moves

A transparent, step-by-step look at exactly what happens from the moment your container arrives at our adda to final delivery.

Overview

One Process, Multiple Paths

Every freight journey with SwiftCargo begins the same way: your container arrives at our central facility. From there, our team takes over — unloading, sorting, and preparing cargo based on its final destination and your preferred transport mode.

Whether you've chosen road, railway, or air, the process is seamless from our end. For door-to-door clients, we handle the entire chain from our adda to your doorstep.

Step by Step

The Full Journey

Here is every stage your cargo goes through — from the moment the container is received to the final signature at your destination.

Container Received

The importer's container arrives at our adda. Our team documents its arrival, inspects the seal, and logs the shipment details before any unloading begins.

1
2

Cargo Unloaded at Adda

Our trained handlers carefully unload all goods from the container. Items are sorted by destination, product type, and assigned transport mode to prepare for dispatch.

Transport Mode Assigned

Based on the client's instructions and cargo type, the appropriate transport mode is confirmed — road, railway, air, or container freight — and dispatch is scheduled.

3
4

Goods Loaded & Dispatched

Cargo is loaded onto the assigned vehicle or bogie and dispatched. For rail, this marks the official start of the freight service. For road, the truck departs for its destination.

In Transit

Cargo is on the move. Our team monitors the shipment and coordinates with drivers, rail operators, or air handlers to ensure everything stays on schedule.

5
6

Delivered & Confirmed

Cargo arrives at the client's factory, warehouse, or specified address. Delivery is confirmed, documentation is completed, and the shipment is officially closed.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions importers ask us most often.

When does the freight service officially start?
+

For railway shipments, the freight service officially begins once goods are loaded into the train bogies and the train departs. For road transport, it begins when the truck leaves our adda.

Do you handle the container unloading yourselves?
+

Yes. Once your container arrives at our adda, our team manages the entire unloading process. You do not need to be present — we document everything and keep you informed.

What types of goods do you transport?
+

We serve importers dealing in a wide range of products — from raw materials and machinery to consumer goods, textiles, and general merchandise. Contact us if you have specific cargo requirements.

What is container freight service exactly?
+

In container freight, we receive your import container, unload the goods, and then reload them into our own managed containers. This gives us better control over handling and allows us to consolidate or split shipments as needed.

Can you deliver to any location in the country?
+

Yes. Our door-to-door service covers deliveries to factories, warehouses, or any specified address across the country. Our road and rail networks give us wide geographic reach.

How do I get started or request a shipment?
+

Simply fill out the inquiry form on our Contact page or call us directly. Share your cargo details, preferred transport mode, and destination — and we'll take care of the rest.
