Service 01

Road Transport

Our road transport service is the backbone of our logistics operation. Once cargo is unloaded and sorted at our adda, our fleet of trucks picks it up and moves it efficiently to its destination — whether across the city or across the country.

  • Direct truck dispatch from our adda facility
  • Experienced drivers with knowledge of national routes
  • Suitable for all cargo sizes and product types
  • Flexible scheduling to match your delivery windows
  • Available for both partial and full truck loads

Service 02

Railway Freight

For large-volume shipments or long-distance routes, railway freight offers a cost-effective and reliable solution. We load goods into train bogies (compartments) and dispatch through the national rail network — this is where our railway freight service officially begins.

  • Goods loaded into dedicated train bogies at our facility
  • Ideal for high-volume, long-haul cargo
  • More economical than road for bulk shipments
  • Scheduled rail dispatch coordination managed by our team
  • Offloading and last-mile delivery at the destination end

1

2

3

Service 04

Container Freight Service

Our container freight service involves receiving goods in the importer's original shipping containers, carefully unloading them, and reloading them into our own managed containers. This ensures better handling, accountability, and streamlined transportation to the final destination.

  • Receive goods from original import containers
  • Careful unloading, inspection, and sorting at our adda
  • Reloaded into SwiftCargo-managed containers
  • Better control over handling and chain of custody
  • Suitable for mixed cargo and multi-destination shipments

Service 05

Warehouse & Adda

Our facility — the adda — is the operational heart of everything we do. It is the first point of contact for every import container we handle. All other services begin here.

  • Secure receipt of all incoming import containers
  • Full unloading by trained handlers
  • Cargo sorted and labelled by destination and mode
  • Short-term holding storage available while dispatch is arranged
  • Staging area for road, rail, and container reload operations

Service 06

Door-to-Door Delivery

Our door-to-door delivery service means exactly that — we take your cargo from our facility all the way to your factory, warehouse, shop floor, or any address you specify. No coordination required on your end; we handle every step.

  • Delivery directly to your factory, warehouse, or site
  • Any address in the country — we arrange the route
  • Coordination across road and last-mile delivery
  • Confirmation and documentation provided at drop-off
  • Available as an add-on to any of our freight services
